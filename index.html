What We Do

We design, build, modernise, and support software platforms for organisations that need practical delivery, not just ideas. Alongside hands-on engineering, we provide technical project management consulting to help clarify scope, plan realistic delivery, manage risk, and keep stakeholders aligned from discovery through to release.

Software & Web Platforms

We build bespoke software, business systems, APIs, and web platforms that solve real operational problems. That includes new green-field products, modern responsive web applications, legacy modernisation, integrations, automation, and long-term platform improvement.

Project Management Consulting

We provide technical project management consulting for software and transformation work, helping teams shape scope, run discovery, prioritise effectively, manage RAID, coordinate stakeholders, and deliver in controlled increments. This is especially useful for organisations modernising legacy systems or needing stronger delivery structure around complex projects.

Workplace Learning

We provide tailored technical training and workplace learning support for development teams, with structured guidance, practical coaching, and feedback that helps people build confidence and capability in real delivery environments.

Scalable Support Solutions

We provide dependable support solutions for organisations that need responsive help alongside ongoing development. From ticket-based support to operational troubleshooting and delivery assistance, we help keep systems moving when it matters most.

Case Studies

A selection of software projects we have designed, built, modernised, and supported across healthcare, operations, HR, insurance, and product data. These examples are here to show the range of problems we help solve, from bespoke platforms and legacy modernisation to open source product intelligence and applied AI prototypes.

Healthcare / NHS collaboration

E-PGME

E-PGME is a postgraduate medical education platform created in collaboration with NHS partners. It helps teams manage teaching events, QR-based attendance tracking, study leave, expense workflows, and administration in one place, with reporting tools that improve visibility and reduce manual overhead.

Visit E-PGME

Help desk / Project management / ITSM

Kribik

Kribik is a platform we designed and built from scratch to bring help desk, project management, and ITSM workflows together in one system. It combines ticketing, approvals, SLA controls, releases, reporting, Git-linked delivery, and AI-assisted support features so teams can manage operational work with more clarity and less tool sprawl.

Visit Kribik

HR / Payroll software

CompactHR

For CompactHR, we developed a cloud-based payroll and HR portal designed to simplify how payroll changes are captured and processed. The system reduces manual rekeying, improves validation, supports employee onboarding, and gives SMEs an easier route into integrated HR and payroll workflows.

Visit CompactHR

Legacy modernisation / Financial services

One Mortgage Systems

For One Mortgage Systems, we provided contracting support on the modernisation of a large legacy ASP.NET platform, helping move core parts of the system onto .NET 8. The work focused on improving maintainability, modern development practices, and the long-term sustainability of a business-critical mortgage application.

Data migration / Search infrastructure

SBS Insurance Services

We have supported SBS Insurance Services with technical delivery work including data migration and Elasticsearch configuration. This helped ensure important data could be moved reliably, structured more effectively, and made easier to search and work with in day-to-day operations.

Visit SBS Insurance Services

Open source / Product intelligence

ProductNormaliser

ProductNormaliser is an open product-intelligence engine we maintain for turning messy retailer and manufacturer data into clean, canonical, comparable product records. It crawls source pages, extracts structured evidence, normalises attributes, resolves identity across sources, and builds an explainable merged product view for downstream analytics and catalogue workflows.

Visit ProductNormaliser

Applied AI / Prototype

AI Medical Scribe

AI Medical Scribe is a browser-based, local-first prototype exploring live consultation transcription, on-device summarisation, document drafting, structured extraction, audit logging, and FHIR export. It shows how privacy-conscious AI tooling can be explored in the browser without relying on a traditional backend.

Visit AI Medical Scribe
